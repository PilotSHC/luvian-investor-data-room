Comp Philosophy

1

Cash: 75–85% of market. We're upfront about this. Below-market cash is the cost of admission to an early-stage rocket ship.

2

Equity: 2–3x market. At a $8M cap, 1% = $80K paper value. At a $28M seed, 1% = $280K. The upside is real.

3

Vesting: standard 4/1. No games. One-year cliff, then monthly. Acceleration on change of control.

4

Revisit at seed. Cash bumps to market rate when seed closes. Early team is rewarded, not punished.

Recruiting Playbook

How we find these people
without a recruiter budget.

👥

Network-First Hiring

Every team member and advisor sends 10 targeted LinkedIn messages. Referral bonus: $5K equity top-up per successful hire.

🎓

INCOSE / MBSE Community

The solutions architect lives in this world. Post in INCOSE chapters, MBSE forums, LinkedIn SE groups. These people want to build the future of their field.

🔥

Counter-Positioning Pitch

"Big tool vendor innovation is dead. We're building what they can't copy. Join now, own 1%+ of the category winner." This pitch resonates with frustrated domain experts.

🌐

Remote-First

No geographic constraints. US time zones preferred but not required. Access the entire talent pool, not just SF/NYC/Austin.

🚀

Speed Kills

Offer within 48hrs of final interview. Skip the multi-week process. Send the SAFE deck to candidates — transparency builds trust.

🔨

Contract-to-Hire

For risky positions (solutions architect, ML eng), start with a 4-week paid project. Reduces bad-hire risk when every dollar counts.

Total recruiting budget: $0. Time investment: 8–10 hrs/week for the founder in Q1–Q2, then delegated to Head of GTM.
