The Pitch — Full Script

Read it. Learn it. Own it.

HOOK Every autonomous system — from self-driving cars to military robots — must be architected, safety-analyzed, and verified before it ships. Today, engineers use tools built twenty years ago: fifteen-thousand-dollar-per-seat desktop software with zero AI, zero collaboration, and no integration across disciplines.

SOLUTION Luvian is the context layer for engineering intelligence. We connect requirements, designs, safety analysis, tests, and telemetry into one continuously evolving knowledge graph — with local-first reasoning that runs entirely on-device for air-gapped defense deployments.

WHY US We're three to ten times cheaper than incumbents like Cameo and DOORS. We're the only platform that gives AI a typed engineering graph to reason over — not a pile of disconnected strings. And our reasoning never leaves the customer's network.

MARKET The MBSE market is four-and-a-half billion dollars, growing fifteen percent annually to ten billion by 2031. We're targeting the fastest-growing slice: autonomous systems programs in defense, automotive, and aerospace.

TRACTION We've built the foundation — the structured-artifact layer of the graph, with AI copilots, real-time collaboration, and four industry demo verticals. We're now entering design partner conversations with robotics, mechatronics, and defense programs.

THE ASK We're raising two-and-a-half million dollars to land our first ten design partners and build the sales engine for the engineering intelligence layer.
