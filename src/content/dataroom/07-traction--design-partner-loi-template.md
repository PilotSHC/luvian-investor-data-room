**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Between:** Luvian, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("**Luvian**")
**And:** [CUSTOMER LEGAL NAME], a [STATE] [ENTITY TYPE] ("**Customer**")

This Letter of Intent ("**LOI**") sets out the parties' mutual intent to enter into a paid design-partner pilot of the Luvian platform. This LOI is **non-binding** except for the sections marked **Binding** below.

---

## 1. Pilot Scope

Customer will deploy and evaluate the Luvian platform within the following scope:

- **Use case:** [e.g. Replace Cameo Systems Modeler for ADAS feature definition on Project [X]]
- **Users:** [N] systems engineers, [M] software engineers, [P] safety engineers
- **Integrations in scope:** [e.g. DOORS export, Polarion test linking, Git push]
- **Industry standards in scope:** [e.g. ISO 26262 ASIL D]
- **Pilot duration:** [60 / 90 / 120] days, beginning on the **Pilot Start Date** (defined below).
- **Deployment model:** [☐ Cloud / ☐ On-premises / ☐ VPC-hosted]

## 2. Pilot Fee

- Customer will pay Luvian a one-time pilot fee of **USD [25,000 / 50,000]**.
- Fee is invoiced on the Pilot Start Date with **Net-30** payment terms.
- Fee is **fully creditable** against the first annual subscription if Customer converts to a commercial agreement within 60 days of pilot end.

## 3. Success Criteria

The pilot will be considered "successful" if all of the following are met by the **Pilot End Date**:

| # | Criterion | Measurement |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | [N] active users with ≥ 1 weekly login | Luvian platform analytics |
| 2 | [Y] models built or migrated representing ≥ [Z] requirements | Luvian platform export |
| 3 | Customer-defined workflow ran end-to-end on Luvian | Joint sign-off |
| 4 | Performance: < 10 min time-to-first-diagram for new users | Luvian + Customer telemetry |
| 5 | Stability: < 1 P1 incident, < 5 P2 incidents over the pilot | Joint incident log |

## 4. Pilot Start Date

The Pilot Start Date is the date on which all of the following have occurred:

- This LOI is fully executed.
- Customer has paid the pilot fee.
- Mutual NDA (or equivalent confidentiality terms) is in effect.
- Customer has provided initial provisioning data (user list, identity provider config, deployment target).

Target Pilot Start Date: **[YYYY-MM-DD]**.

## 5. Conversion to Annual Contract

If success criteria are met, the parties will negotiate in good faith an annual subscription agreement on the following indicative terms (subject to a definitive agreement):

- **Annual fee:** USD [100,000 – 250,000] depending on user count and deployment model
- **Term:** 12 months, auto-renewing absent 60-day notice
- **SLA:** 99.9% availability for cloud; mutually agreed for on-premises
- **Support:** Business hours (cloud) / 24×7 P1 (on-premises)
- **Pilot fee credit:** Full pilot fee credited against the first annual fee

Either party may decline to convert. This LOI does not obligate either party to sign an annual agreement.

## 6. Customer References (Binding)

Provided the pilot is successful, Customer agrees that Luvian may:

- Identify Customer as a "design partner" in investor materials, on its website, and in press materials, **with Customer's prior approval of the specific wording**.
- Use a mutually agreed-upon case study or quote.

Customer will respond to Luvian's reference-call requests from prospective customers and investors **in good faith**, subject to Customer's reasonable scheduling.

## 7. Confidentiality (Binding)

Each party will treat the other's Confidential Information consistent with the **Mutual NDA** between the parties dated **[YYYY-MM-DD]** (or, if no such NDA exists, the confidentiality obligations attached as Exhibit A). For the avoidance of doubt:

- Customer's models, requirements, and engineering data are Customer's Confidential Information.
- Luvian's pricing, roadmap, and unreleased features are Luvian's Confidential Information.

## 8. Data Protection (Binding)

If Customer uploads any personal data, the parties will execute Luvian's **Data Processing Addendum** (in `_pdf_exports/Data_Processing_Addendum.pdf`) before Pilot Start Date.

## 9. IP Ownership (Binding)

- Customer retains all rights to its models, requirements, and engineering data.
- Luvian retains all rights to the platform.
- Feedback and suggestions provided by Customer are licensed to Luvian on a non-exclusive, royalty-free, perpetual basis.

## 10. Term and Termination (Binding)

- This LOI is effective on the date of the last signature below.
- Either party may terminate the pilot for material breach with 15 days' written notice if the breach is not cured.
- Sections 6, 7, 8, 9, and 10 survive termination.

## 11. Governing Law (Binding)

This LOI is governed by the laws of the State of **[Delaware / California]**, excluding conflict-of-laws rules. Disputes will be resolved in the state and federal courts located in **[San Francisco County, California]**.

## 12. Non-Binding Effect

Other than the Binding sections noted above, this LOI is a **non-binding statement of mutual intent**. No legal obligation arises from the non-binding sections until the parties sign a definitive pilot agreement or commercial subscription agreement.
