## Speaker Notes (selected)

### Slide 2. The Problem

> *"MBSE tools suck. If you compare them to tools in other industries, they would be ridiculed."*
> — Pari Singh

Cameo Systems Modeler is the market leader. It is a 9 GB Java desktop app with a UX that predates Slack. Adoption rates across surveyed engineering teams hover around 5%. The market is not underserved. It is hostile to the people it serves.

### Slide 3. Why Now

Three converging forces.

1. **SysML v2** breaks every incumbent's data model. Their migration costs are higher than our from-scratch costs.
2. **LLMs** are now good enough to author, validate, and refactor structured engineering models. The modeling tax is finally collapsing.
3. **Browser-first** is already complete in every adjacent category (CAD, EDA, ALM). MBSE is the last holdout.

### Slide 9. Competition (7 Powers)

| Power | How Luvian wins |
|---|---|
| Counter-positioning | Browser-native plus real-time is strictly worse for incumbents' existing customer base. They cannot copy without cannibalizing seat revenue. |
| Switching costs | SysML v2 lock-in is their problem, not ours. We are the migration target. |
| Process power | AI-native authoring and maturity gates that incumbents cannot retrofit. |
| Cornered resource | Founding-team domain credibility (enterprise, autonomy). |
| Network economies | Real-time collab leads to multi-user, then team adoption, then org adoption. |

### Slide 14. The Ask

* $1.5M to $2.0M Convertible Note at the LLC stage, pre-Delaware-Flip.
* Valuation cap: TBD with lead. See [Convertible_Note_Template.md](../10_Appendix/Convertible_Note_Template.md).
* 5% simple interest. 24-month maturity. Auto-converts on Qualified Financing or on the LLC to Delaware C-corp conversion, whichever is first.
* Use of funds explicitly includes the Delaware Flip and the recruitment of two engineering co-founders plus one founding GTM hire.
* 18-month runway to a Series A milestone bundle:
  * Delaware Flip complete (LLC reorganized as Luvian, Inc.)
  * Founding team in place (3 hires beyond Stephan)
  * 3 paying design-partner pilots (at least $100K ARR-equivalent each)
  * 1 ASIL-rated reference deployment
  * On-prem packaging proven across macOS, Linux, Windows
  * Multi-persona surface area shipped (SE, software dev, safety analyst, PM)
