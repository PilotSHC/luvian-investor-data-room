**Executed.** The first formal action of the sole member of Luvian Labs LLC, taken by written consent in lieu of a meeting at the time of formation.

**Download the executed PDF using the button above.**

## What it authorised

| Action | Effect |
|---|---|
| Adoption of the Operating Agreement | Confirms the single-member operating agreement of Luvian Labs LLC. |
| Confirmation of sole member and 100% membership interest | Stephan Claxton is the sole member; 100% of the membership interest is issued to him. |
| Authority to open business banking and obtain an EIN | Authorises the founder, acting as the Member, to open the company's bank account and to obtain a Federal Employer Identification Number (EIN). |
| IP assignment | Approves and accepts the IP Assignment Agreement transferring all founder IP into the company. |
| Authority to issue Convertible Notes | Pre-authorises the issuance of Convertible Notes to investors, on terms approved by the Member, without further consent. |
| Authority to engage counsel | Confirms the Member's authority to retain outside counsel, accountants, and registered-agent services on behalf of the company. |
| Authority to execute the Delaware Flip | Pre-authorises the LLC → Delaware C-corp Reorganization on the Member's election. |

This document is what investor counsel asks for to confirm that every subsequent action, opening Mercury, signing Notes, executing IP assignments, was taken under proper authority.

## Effect after the Delaware Flip

On the Reorganization, this consent is preserved in the company's records as part of the LLC's historical minute book. The successor Delaware corporation will adopt its own initial board consent at the time the Reorganization closes, governed by Delaware General Corporation Law (DGCL).
