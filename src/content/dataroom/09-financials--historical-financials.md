## Year-to-date Operating Position

The dollar values for the four months between formation and pre-seed close are intentionally minor — that is the point of operating lean pre-revenue. Specifics:

- **Revenue:** none, by design. Pre-revenue category-creation play.
- **Operating expenses:** dominated by formation costs (LLC filing, registered agent, IP assignment, NDAs, banking setup) plus a low-thousands monthly infrastructure run rate (Vercel, Supabase, Hocuspocus, foundation-model API spend through the AI Gateway, GitHub / Linear / Notion / Slack tooling, QuickBooks plus a fractional bookkeeper, lumpy legal).
- **Founder compensation:** zero cash. Founders are on equity-only economics captured in the executed Founder IP Assignment and the existing-team CIIA arrangements. Post-round, founders move onto a market-floor cash-plus-equity package modelled in the *Financial Model Summary* and the editable CSV.
- **Cash on hand:** funded out of founder personal capital pending close. Specific balance is shared 1:1 with the Lead on the first call.

A consolidated monthly P&L (QuickBooks export) and the editable model are available to the Lead on request.
