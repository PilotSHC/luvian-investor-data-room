## 5. What we are *not* showing (and why)

We deliberately do not surface here:

- **Logo waterfalls** of companies whose engineers happened to take a call.
- **MRR or revenue charts** that don't exist yet.
- **Cohort growth curves** for a product that isn't generally available.
- **Magic-quadrant-style pseudo-rankings** versus competitors.
- **Customer testimonials** from people who are not real customers.

That is the kind of fake traction that infrastructure-stage investors learn to discount. We don't pretend to it.
