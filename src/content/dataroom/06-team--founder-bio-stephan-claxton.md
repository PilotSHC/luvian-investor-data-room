**Luvian Labs LLC · Pre-Seed · 2026-05 · Confidential**

Two co-founders plus our first software engineer. Each owns a clear surface of the company, and the round funds the next three hires beyond the current team.

## Stephan Claxton — Founder & CEO

Systems engineer and autonomy architect focused on operational context, traceability, and AI-enabled engineering workflows across regulated industries.

* Background in autonomy systems, MBSE, and systems architecture
* Experience spanning aerospace, automotive, and enterprise engineering workflows
* Deep expertise in engineering traceability, ontology design, and operational context modeling
* Formerly worked on advanced autonomy and engineering infrastructure initiatives at Applied Intuition
* Building Luvian to enable trustworthy AI reasoning across complex engineering organizations

The combination of MBSE depth, defense-program delivery experience, and AV verification work places this founder at the intersection of three regulated industries that almost no one else has lived inside. That cross-domain fluency is the founder–market fit thesis.

[stephanclaxton.com](https://www.stephanclaxton.com/) · [stephan@luvian.info](mailto:stephan@luvian.info) · Sunnyvale, CA

## Jarred Gou — Chief Product Officer

Product leader translating Luvian's thesis into a coherent, investor-legible product roadmap and a buildable platform plan.

* Sets product strategy, roadmap, and the SysML v2 conformance plan
* Leads engineering architecture across the three-layer rendering pipeline and the AI integration surface
* Owns the product narrative for fundraising and design-partner conversations
* Drives the multi-persona product surface for systems engineers, software developers, safety analysts, and program managers
* Works tightly with Stephan on category positioning and with Colin on the deployment posture for regulated customers

The product thesis Luvian is built on, that the next generation of MBSE tooling must be AI-native, browser-first, and collaborative by default, is Jarred's. That conviction shapes every prioritization call inside the company.

## Colin Zhang — Software Engineer

Enterprise-infrastructure engineer responsible for the platform's deployment posture in the customer environments Luvian sells into.

* Owns the platform's cross-platform foundation (macOS, Linux, Windows; cloud and on-premises)
* Leads authentication and authorization architecture (OIDC, SAML, LDAP) for enterprise tenants
* Brings hands-on experience from VMware, where he worked on virtualization and cloud-native platform technology at massive scale
* Translates large-enterprise procurement, IT-governance, and security expectations into product requirements
* Sets the operational rigor required to serve defense, aerospace, and automotive customers

The credibility to walk into a defense prime's IT review and have the right conversation about on-prem, air-gapped, and FIPS-validated deployment lives with Colin.
