**RESOLVED**, that the **Luvian, Inc. 20[YY] Equity Incentive Plan**, in the form attached as **Exhibit C**, is hereby adopted, effective as of the Effective Date, and that **[NUMBER — e.g. 450,000]** shares of Common Stock are reserved for issuance under the Plan.

**RESOLVED**, that the Company shall open one or more deposit accounts at **[BANK_NAME — e.g. Mercury, Brex Cash, Silicon Valley Bank, JPMorgan Chase]**.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that any one of the **CEO, COO/CTO, or CFO** is hereby authorized, on behalf of the Company, to (a) execute account-opening documentation, (b) deposit funds, (c) issue checks, wire transfers, and ACH payments, and (d) sign such other instruments as may be required by the bank.

### 8. Engagement of Service Providers

**RESOLVED**, that the officers are authorized to engage on the Company's behalf:
- Outside legal counsel of record (e.g. Cooley LLP, Gunderson Dettmer, Wilson Sonsini, Stripe Atlas Legal, Clerky, or another firm).
- An outside accountant / bookkeeper (e.g. Pilot, Bench, or QuickBooks Online with internal bookkeeping).
- A registered agent in Delaware.
- An equity-management platform (e.g. Carta, Pulley, Capbase).
- An identity provider, payroll provider, and any other service necessary for ordinary business operations.

### 9. Tax and Regulatory Filings

**RESOLVED**, that the officers are authorized and directed to (a) apply for and obtain a federal Employer Identification Number (EIN); (b) make all required state and federal tax registrations; (c) make foreign-qualification filings in any state where the Company has nexus; and (d) take such other tax, securities, and regulatory actions as may be necessary or appropriate to commence business operations.

### 10. Ratification of Pre-Incorporation Actions

**RESOLVED**, that all acts taken on behalf of the Company prior to the date of incorporation by the founders, the incorporator, or any other person, including the execution of agreements, the receipt or payment of money, the engagement of service providers, and the development of intellectual property in respect of the Company's business, are hereby ratified and adopted as the acts of the Company.

### 11. Form of Stock Certificate

**RESOLVED**, that the Company shall record stock issuances in book-entry form. To the extent any stockholder requests a physical certificate, the form attached as **Exhibit E** is hereby adopted.

### 12. General Authorization

**RESOLVED**, that the officers are authorized to take such further action and execute such further documents as they may deem necessary or appropriate to carry out the intent of these resolutions, and any such action heretofore taken is hereby ratified and approved.
