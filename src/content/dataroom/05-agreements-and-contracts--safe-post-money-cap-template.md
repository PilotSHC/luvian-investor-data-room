---# SAFE — Post-Money Valuation Cap (YC Standard)

> ## ⚠️ POST-FLIP TEMPLATE — NOT USABLE WHILE LUVIAN IS AN LLC
>
> SAFEs convert into **preferred stock**, which an LLC does not have. **Do not execute this template** while the issuer is **Luvian Labs LLC**.
>
> Pre-flip path: use the **Convertible Promissory Note** template at `Convertible_Note_TEMPLATE.md` — that note has a built-in conversion trigger on the planned LLC → Delaware C-corp Reorganization (see `../04_Legal_and_Corporate/LLC_to_Ccorp_Conversion_Plan.md`).
>
> **This YC SAFE becomes the standard fundraising instrument _after_ the Reorganization closes** and the issuer becomes **Luvian, Inc.** (Delaware C-corp).

> **DRAFT TEMPLATE — modeled on the YC Post-Money Valuation Cap SAFE.** This is the most common pre-seed instrument. The "no-edit zones" of the YC SAFE are sacred — investors will reject material changes. The only commercial terms to fill in are: **investor name, purchase amount, post-money valuation cap, MFN flag, pro-rata side letter (separate doc), and date.** Always pull the latest YC SAFE PDF from [ycombinator.com/documents](https://www.ycombinator.com/documents) before each closing. **Review with counsel.** See `../04_Legal_and_Corporate/_LEGAL_DISCLAIMER.md`.

## SIMPLE AGREEMENT FOR FUTURE EQUITY (POST-MONEY VALUATION CAP)

**THIS INSTRUMENT** and the rights granted hereunder have not been registered under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended ("**Securities Act**"), or under the securities laws of any state. This Instrument has been acquired for investment and may not be sold or transferred without compliance with applicable securities laws.

---

**LUVIAN, INC.**

**SAFE — Simple Agreement for Future Equity**
*(Post-Money Valuation Cap, no Discount)*

THIS CERTIFIES THAT in exchange for the payment by **[INVESTOR_LEGAL_NAME]** ("**Investor**") of **$[PURCHASE_AMOUNT]** (the "**Purchase Amount**") on or about **[CLOSING_DATE]**, **Luvian, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "**Company**"), issues to Investor the right to certain shares of the Company's Capital Stock, subject to the terms set forth below.

The "**Post-Money Valuation Cap**" is **$[POST_MONEY_CAP].**

See **Section 2** for certain additional defined terms.

---

### 1. Events

(a) **Equity Financing.** If there is an Equity Financing before the termination of this SAFE, on the initial closing of such Equity Financing, this SAFE will automatically convert into the number of shares of **Safe Preferred Stock** equal to the Purchase Amount divided by the **Conversion Price**.

In connection with the automatic conversion of this SAFE, Investor will execute and deliver to the Company all of the transaction documents related to the Equity Financing, provided that such documents are the same as those entered into with the Standard Preferred Stock investors, with appropriate variations for the Safe Preferred Stock and on the same terms applicable to investors with the most favorable terms in respect of conversion or pre-existing rights.

(b) **Liquidity Event.** If there is a Liquidity Event before the termination of this SAFE, this SAFE will automatically be entitled (subject to the liquidation priority set forth in Section 1(d)) at the option of Investor, to receive either (i) cash payment equal to the Purchase Amount (the "**Cash-Out Amount**") or (ii) automatically converted into the number of shares of Common Stock equal to the Purchase Amount divided by the **Liquidity Price**.

(c) **Dissolution Event.** If there is a Dissolution Event before this SAFE terminates, the Company will pay an amount equal to the Cash-Out Amount, due and payable to Investor immediately prior to, or concurrently with, the consummation of the Dissolution Event.

(d) **Liquidation Priority.** In a Liquidity Event or Dissolution Event:
- This SAFE is **junior** to any outstanding indebtedness and trade payables.
- This SAFE is **on par with** all other SAFEs.
- This SAFE is **senior to** any then-outstanding Common Stock.

(e) **Termination.** This SAFE terminates upon either (i) the issuance of shares to Investor pursuant to Section 1(a) or 1(b)(ii), or (ii) the payment, or setting aside of payment, of amounts due Investor pursuant to Section 1(b)(i) or 1(c).

### 2. Definitions

- **"Capital Stock"** means the capital stock of the Company, including, without limitation, the **Common Stock** and the **Preferred Stock**.
- **"Common Stock"** means the common stock of the Company, par value $0.00001 per share.
- **"Company Capitalization"** is calculated as of immediately prior to the Equity Financing and (without double-counting), in each case, on an as-converted basis: includes all issued and outstanding shares of Capital Stock, all outstanding options, all outstanding promised options, and the Unissued Option Pool. Excludes other Safes (i.e. SAFEs are calculated separately into the Conversion Price).
- **"Conversion Price"** means the **SAFE Price**.
- **"Discount Price"** — *not applicable; no discount.*
- **"Dissolution Event"** means (i) a voluntary termination of operations, (ii) a general assignment for the benefit of creditors, or (iii) any other liquidation, dissolution, or winding up of the Company (excluding a Liquidity Event), whether voluntary or involuntary.
- **"Equity Financing"** means a bona fide transaction or series of transactions with the principal purpose of raising capital, pursuant to which the Company issues and sells Preferred Stock at a fixed valuation, including but not limited to a pre-money or post-money valuation.
- **"Initial Price"** means the price per share of the Standard Preferred Stock sold in the Equity Financing.
- **"Liquidity Capitalization"** is calculated as of immediately prior to the Liquidity Event and includes all issued and outstanding shares of Capital Stock, vested options, and Safes other than this SAFE.
- **"Liquidity Event"** means a Change of Control or an Initial Public Offering.
- **"Liquidity Price"** means $[POST_MONEY_CAP] divided by the Liquidity Capitalization.
- **"Post-Money Valuation Cap"** is $[POST_MONEY_CAP].
- **"Pro Rata Rights Agreement"** — *separate document; see `Pro_Rata_Side_Letter_TEMPLATE.md`.*
- **"SAFE Price"** means $[POST_MONEY_CAP] divided by the Company Capitalization.
- **"Safe Preferred Stock"** means the shares of the series of Preferred Stock issued to Investor in the Equity Financing, having the identical rights, privileges, preferences, and restrictions as the shares of Standard Preferred Stock, other than with respect to: (i) the per-share liquidation amount and the conversion price, which will be equal to the SAFE Price; and (ii) the basis for any dividends, which will be based on the SAFE Price.
- **"Standard Preferred Stock"** means the shares of Preferred Stock issued to investors investing new money in the Company in connection with the Equity Financing.
- **"Unissued Option Pool"** means all shares of Capital Stock that are reserved, available for future grant, and not subject to any outstanding options or promised options.

### 3. Company Representations

The Company represents and warrants that:
- It is a corporation duly organized, validly existing, and in good standing under the laws of Delaware.
- The execution, delivery, and performance of this SAFE has been duly authorized by all necessary corporate action.
- This SAFE is its legal, valid, and binding obligation, enforceable against it.
- No consents, approvals, or filings are required other than those that have been obtained or that will be made in due course (e.g. Form D, blue-sky filings).
- It will use the Purchase Amount for working capital and general corporate purposes.

### 4. Investor Representations

Investor represents and warrants that:
- It has full legal capacity, power, and authority to execute this SAFE.
- This SAFE is its legal, valid, and binding obligation, enforceable against it.
- It is an **accredited investor** as defined in Rule 501 of Regulation D under the Securities Act, or otherwise qualifies for an exemption from registration.
- It is purchasing the SAFE for its own account, for investment, and not with a view to distribution.
- It has had access to all information about the Company that it has requested.

### 5. Most Favored Nation (MFN) — *(check box if applicable)*

**☐ MFN Election.** If the Company issues a SAFE to a subsequent investor with provisions more favorable to such investor (other than purchase amount and post-money valuation cap), Investor may elect to amend this SAFE to incorporate such more-favorable terms. *(Standard for angel-tier investors. Lead investors typically do not need MFN because they set the terms.)*

### 6. Miscellaneous

(a) Any provision of this SAFE may be amended, waived, or modified by written consent of the Company and either (i) Investor or (ii) the majority-in-interest of all SAFEs with the same "Post-Money Valuation Cap" and the same "Discount Rate" (for grouping purposes, treat each cap-discount pair as a separate group).

(b) Any notice required or permitted by this SAFE will be deemed sufficient when delivered personally or by overnight courier, or 48 hours after deposit in the U.S. mail, certified, addressed to the address listed below.

(c) Investor is not entitled, as a holder of this SAFE, to vote or be deemed a holder of Capital Stock for any purpose other than tax purposes, nor will anything in this SAFE be construed to confer on Investor, as such, any rights of a stockholder of the Company.

(d) Neither this SAFE nor the rights contained herein may be assigned, by operation of law or otherwise, by either party without the prior written consent of the other; provided, however, that this SAFE and/or the rights contained herein may be assigned without the Company's consent by Investor to any other entity who directly or indirectly controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with Investor.

(e) In the event any one or more of the provisions of this SAFE is for any reason held to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, in whole or in part or in any respect, the validity of the remaining provisions will not in any way be affected.

(f) All rights and obligations hereunder will be governed by the laws of the State of **[Delaware / California]**, without regard to the conflicts-of-laws provisions thereof.

(g) The parties acknowledge and agree that for United States federal and state income tax purposes this SAFE is, and at all times has been, intended to be characterized as **stock**, and more particularly as Common Stock for purposes of Sections 1202 and 1045 of the Internal Revenue Code. Accordingly, the parties agree to treat this SAFE consistent with the foregoing intent for all U.S. federal and state income-tax purposes (including, without limitation, on their respective tax returns or other informational statements).

## Drafting Notes (delete before signing)

- **Pull the latest official YC SAFE PDF before every closing** at [ycombinator.com/documents](https://www.ycombinator.com/documents). The above is a transcription for working purposes — the official YC PDF (with no edits to non-blue-text portions) is the document you actually execute.
- The only commercial terms to negotiate are **Purchase Amount, Post-Money Valuation Cap, and MFN/Pro-Rata flags**. Resist edits anywhere else.
- For pro-rata rights, use a separate **Pro-Rata Side Letter** (`Pro_Rata_Side_Letter_TEMPLATE.md`) — typically only for $250K+ checks.
- Confirm with counsel whether **Form D** must be filed within 15 days of the first sale (Reg D 506(b) or 506(c)). Most pre-seed SAFE rounds qualify under § 4(a)(2) without Form D, but verify.
- After execution: deposit funds, update Carta, file the executed SAFE in `_private/Contracts/Funding/SAFE_<investor>_<date>_EXECUTED.pdf`, update `Funding_Documents_INDEX.md` with: status = ✅ executed (date), amount, cap.
