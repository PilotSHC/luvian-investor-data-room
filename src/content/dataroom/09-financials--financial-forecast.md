## 4. Sensitivity, what changes the picture

### Faster founding hires (months 1–2)

If the GTM Lead and the Founding ML and AI Engineer both close in the
first 60 days, Q2 burn picks up earlier, but milestone ramp also
accelerates by about one quarter. Series A prep starts in month 14
rather than month 16. Net runway impact: neutral; net Series A timing:
positive.

### Slower founding hires (months 4–6)

If founding hires slip by two to three months, Q2 burn is lower than
planned (extending runway by about 1.5 months) but **milestone risk
increases**. The architecture-document and design-partner-deployment
milestones depend on the founding team being in place. Net runway
impact: positive; net milestone risk: meaningful.

This is the highest-leverage sensitivity. The lead investor's
recruiting help directly de-risks it.

### One design partner converts to paid before Series A

At pre-seed it is plausible that one of the 2–3 design partners signs a
paid deployment ($500K–$1.5M range, strategic-account ACV under the
new category framing) before the Series A closes. We do **not** budget
this. If it lands, it is bonus runway and stronger Series-A material.
neither the round size nor the milestone plan depend on it.

### Faster compute/model price decline

Foundation-model API costs have been declining 50–70% per year for
equivalent capability since 2023. We model the compute + model bucket
as flat for safety; the realistic outcome is that this bucket shrinks
over the round, contributing 1–2 months of additional runway.

## 6. Series A bridge planning

The Series A is contemplated **12–18 months** from pre-seed close,
priced into the post-Flip Delaware C-corp. Target round size: $8M–$15M.

The key Series-A trigger is **typed-context-graph reference deployments
+ category proof**, not revenue scale:

- 2–3 strategic accounts in production with provenance, suspect-link
  propagation, and air-gap-deployment visible end-to-end.
- Reference architecture publicly documented and battle-tested in at
  least one IL5/IL6-adjacent customer environment.
- Cross-artifact unification across requirements, models, simulation,
  and test data demonstrated at one partner, the moat under the new
  category.
- 6-person team operating without the founder as the bottleneck on any
  workstream.
- Clean post-Flip cap table.

Pre-seed investors' pro-rata participation in the Series A is a
contemplated and capital-planned outcome, see Investor Q&A, Q15.

## 7. What we are honest about

- This is a **plan**, not a guarantee. Pre-seed plans are revisited
  every 90 days against actual milestone progress.
- The round size is **deliberately conservative**. We are sized to be
  rational, not to be impressive.
- The runway buffer is **deliberately tight**. We are not raising for
  optionality; we are raising for execution.
- We are **pre-revenue throughout the planning horizon**. We do not
  plan around revenue we have not signed.
