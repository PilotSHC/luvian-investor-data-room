## Why we are raising now

Four forcing functions converged in 2024–2026: 95% of enterprise GenAI pilots returned zero ROI on $30–40B invested (MIT NANDA, Jul 2025), $13.8B flowed into robotics in 2025 vs $7.8B in 2024, the DoD MOSA mandate (Dec 2024) put open architectures on the critical path of $384.3B in MDAP spend, and IL5/IL6 air-gap stopped being a feature request and became an architectural requirement that locks SaaS context AI out structurally. The window to define the *engineering intelligence layer* category is roughly 18–24 months before incumbents and horizontal-AI players catch up.

The full reasoning is in *05 Market Thesis/Why Now Memo*. The architecture thesis is in *04 Technical Architecture/Architecture Vision Memo*.

We are raising the minimum capital required to (a) complete the founding team with three named full-time hires, (b) put the architecture into production at 2–3 design partners with provenance and suspect-link propagation visible end-to-end, and (c) reach a Series-A-credible reference story.

---

## What we are not optimising for

We are deliberately **not** raising more than we need.

- We are not optimising round size for headline value.
- We are not racing to a high valuation cap that constrains the next
  round.
- We are not chasing a name lead at the expense of an active lead.
- We are not building a sales motion before the substrate is ready.

This is a focused architecture round. We want a partner who underwrites
the architecture bet, not the GTM motion.

## What we want from the lead

In priority order:

1. **Conviction in the architecture thesis.** The single thing that
   matters. Read the architecture memo, push back on it, decide.
2. **Active partnership.** Recruiting (especially the GTM Lead and
   the Founding ML and AI Engineer), introductions to two or three
   design-partner engineering leaders, and a board-equivalent role
   pre-flip.
3. **Follow-on capital capacity.** Plan to participate at the next
   round on pro-rata or super-pro-rata.
4. **Domain or operator depth.** Either AI infra / dev tools / regulated
   SaaS background, or operator experience in autonomy / aerospace /
   defence engineering organisations.

We are not running a fast-cycle process. We are looking for the right
lead, not the first lead.
