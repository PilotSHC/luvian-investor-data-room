## MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This **Master Services Agreement** ("**MSA**") is entered into as of **[YYYY-MM-DD]** (the "**Effective Date**") between:

- **Luvian, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation, with its principal place of business at **[ADDRESS]** ("**Luvian**" or "**Provider**"); and
- **[CUSTOMER_LEGAL_NAME]**, a **[STATE / COUNTRY] [ENTITY TYPE]**, with its principal place of business at **[ADDRESS]** ("**Customer**").

### 1. Services and Order Forms

(a) **Services.** Luvian will provide Customer access to the Luvian software-as-a-service platform and related professional services as set forth in one or more **Order Forms** signed under this MSA.

(b) **Order Forms.** Each Order Form will set forth: subscription tier, number of users, deployment model (cloud / VPC / on-premises), term, fees, and any custom terms.

(c) **Pilot Order Form.** A pilot Order Form will follow the structure set forth in **Exhibit A — Pilot Form**.

### 2. Access and Use

(a) **License Grant.** Subject to this MSA and payment of fees, Luvian grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable, worldwide right to access and use the Services during the Subscription Term, solely for Customer's internal business purposes.

(b) **Permitted Users.** Customer may permit its employees, contractors, and authorized representatives to use the Services, subject to compliance with this MSA. Customer is responsible for the acts and omissions of its users.

(c) **Restrictions.** Customer will not (and will not permit any third party to): (i) reverse-engineer, decompile, or disassemble the Services; (ii) interfere with the Services' operation; (iii) use the Services to build a competing product; (iv) sublicense, resell, or distribute the Services; or (v) use the Services in violation of applicable law.

### 3. Customer Data and Confidentiality

(a) **Customer Data.** "**Customer Data**" means all data, content, models, requirements, and information uploaded to or processed by the Services on Customer's behalf. Customer retains all right, title, and interest in Customer Data.

(b) **License to Process.** Customer grants Luvian a non-exclusive, worldwide license to host, process, and display Customer Data solely as necessary to provide the Services and to comply with applicable law.

(c) **Data Processing Addendum.** If Customer Data includes personal data, the parties will execute the **Data Processing Addendum** attached as **Exhibit B**.

(d) **Confidentiality.** Each party will protect the other's Confidential Information consistent with the Mutual NDA between the parties (or, if none, the confidentiality terms set forth in **Exhibit C**).

### 4. Fees and Payment

(a) **Fees.** Fees are set forth in each Order Form and are payable in U.S. dollars.

(b) **Invoicing.** Luvian will invoice Customer per the Order Form. Unless otherwise stated, fees are due **Net-30** from invoice date.

(c) **Late Payment.** Undisputed past-due amounts accrue interest at the lesser of **1.5% per month** or the maximum permitted by law.

(d) **Taxes.** Fees are exclusive of taxes. Customer is responsible for all sales, use, VAT, GST, and similar taxes, except for taxes based on Luvian's net income.

### 5. Term and Termination

(a) **MSA Term.** This MSA begins on the Effective Date and continues until terminated.

(b) **Subscription Term.** Each Order Form has its own term as specified therein. Subscriptions auto-renew for successive 12-month terms unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least **60 days** before the renewal date.

(c) **Termination for Cause.** Either party may terminate this MSA or any Order Form for material breach if the breach is not cured within **30 days** of written notice.

(d) **Effect of Termination.** Upon termination: (i) all rights granted to Customer terminate; (ii) Customer's outstanding fees become immediately due; and (iii) Luvian will, on Customer's request within 30 days, make Customer Data available for export, after which Luvian may delete it.

(e) **Survival.** Sections 3 (Customer Data and Confidentiality), 4 (Fees), 6 (Warranties), 7 (Indemnification), 8 (Limitation of Liability), and 10 (General) survive termination.

### 6. Warranties; Disclaimers

(a) **Mutual Warranties.** Each party represents that (i) it has full power and authority to enter into this MSA, and (ii) its performance will not violate applicable law or any contract to which it is a party.

(b) **Service Warranty.** Luvian warrants that the Services will materially conform to the documentation. Customer's exclusive remedy for breach of this warranty is for Luvian to use commercially reasonable efforts to correct the non-conformity, or, if it cannot, to refund the unused portion of pre-paid fees.

(c) **Disclaimer.** EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET FORTH ABOVE, THE SERVICES ARE PROVIDED "**AS IS**" AND LUVIAN DISCLAIMS ALL OTHER WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, AND NON-INFRINGEMENT. LUVIAN DOES NOT WARRANT THAT THE SERVICES WILL BE UNINTERRUPTED OR ERROR-FREE.

### 7. Indemnification

(a) **By Luvian.** Luvian will defend Customer against any third-party claim that the Services infringe a U.S. patent, copyright, or trademark, and pay damages awarded against Customer (or settlement amounts approved by Luvian). Luvian's obligations do not apply to: (i) Customer Data; (ii) modifications not made by Luvian; or (iii) use of the Services in combination with other products not provided by Luvian if the infringement would not have occurred but for the combination.

(b) **By Customer.** Customer will defend Luvian against any third-party claim arising from Customer Data or Customer's use of the Services in violation of this MSA.

(c) **Procedure.** The indemnified party will (i) give prompt notice of the claim, (ii) give the indemnifying party sole control of the defense and settlement, and (iii) cooperate at the indemnifying party's expense.

### 8. Limitation of Liability

(a) **Cap.** EXCEPT FOR THE PARTIES' INDEMNIFICATION OBLIGATIONS UNDER SECTION 7 AND CUSTOMER'S PAYMENT OBLIGATIONS, EACH PARTY'S AGGREGATE LIABILITY UNDER THIS MSA IS LIMITED TO **THE FEES PAID OR PAYABLE BY CUSTOMER UNDER THE APPLICABLE ORDER FORM IN THE 12 MONTHS PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING RISE TO LIABILITY**.

(b) **Exclusion.** NEITHER PARTY IS LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, CONSEQUENTIAL, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, EXEMPLARY, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES (INCLUDING LOST PROFITS) ARISING OUT OF OR RELATING TO THIS MSA, EVEN IF ADVISED OF THE POSSIBILITY OF SUCH DAMAGES.

### 9. Service Levels and Support

Service-level commitments and support obligations are set forth in **Exhibit D** (or in the applicable Order Form if differing).

### 10. General

(a) **Governing Law; Venue.** This MSA is governed by the laws of the State of **[Delaware / California]**. The parties consent to the exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in **[San Francisco County, California]**.

(b) **Notices.** Notices must be in writing and delivered by email (with delivery confirmation) or overnight courier to the address listed above. Notices are effective upon receipt.

(c) **Force Majeure.** Neither party is liable for delays or failures caused by events beyond its reasonable control (acts of God, war, terrorism, governmental orders, internet outages, etc.).

(d) **Independent Contractors.** The parties are independent contractors. Nothing in this MSA creates a partnership, agency, or employment relationship.

(e) **Assignment.** Neither party may assign this MSA without the other's prior written consent, except either party may assign without consent in connection with a merger, reorganization, or sale of all or substantially all of its assets. Any non-permitted assignment is void.

(f) **Entire Agreement; Order of Precedence.** This MSA, together with any Order Form and exhibits, constitutes the entire agreement and supersedes prior or contemporaneous agreements. In the event of a conflict, the order of precedence is: (1) the Order Form, (2) the exhibits, (3) this MSA.

(g) **Amendment; Waiver.** Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties. No waiver is effective unless in writing.

(h) **Severability.** If any provision is unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in effect.

(i) **Counterparts; Electronic Signature.** This MSA may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature.

(j) **Publicity.** Neither party will issue any press release or public announcement about this MSA without the other party's prior written consent. Subject to that, Luvian may include Customer's name and logo in lists of customers on its website and in investor materials.

## Drafting Notes (delete before signing)

- For automotive / aerospace / defense customers, expect ASIL / DO-178C / MIL-STD compliance language in their procurement attachments. Do not accept any open-ended indemnification.
- For EU customers: GDPR DPA and SCCs are required (Exhibit B).
- For US federal customers: requires FAR / DFARS clauses; do not sign without counsel review.
- Cap on liability at "12 months of fees" is standard at SaaS scale; some enterprise customers demand higher caps for security incidents — negotiate carefully.
- Confirm publicity rights with Stephan / GTM before signing — design-partner logos drive fundraising.
