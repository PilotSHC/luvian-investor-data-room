## CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION AND INVENTION ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT

This **Confidential Information and Invention Assignment Agreement** (this "**Agreement**") is entered into as of **[YYYY-MM-DD]** between **Luvian, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "**Company**"), and **[NAME]** ("**I**" or "**me**").

### Recitals

WHEREAS, I am being engaged by the Company as an **[employee / consultant / contractor / officer / director]**; and

WHEREAS, the Company has agreed to engage me on the condition that I sign this Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### 1. Confidential Information

(a) **Definition.** "**Confidential Information**" means all non-public information disclosed to me, or to which I have access, that relates to the Company's business, including but not limited to: technical data, trade secrets, know-how, research, product plans, products, services, customer lists, supplier lists, software, algorithms, designs, source code, financial information, marketing plans, and any other information identified as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential.

(b) **Exclusions.** Confidential Information does not include information that (i) is publicly known and made generally available through no wrongful act of mine; (ii) was known to me prior to its disclosure by the Company without an obligation of confidentiality; (iii) is rightfully received from a third party without restriction; or (iv) I can demonstrate was independently developed without use of or reference to Confidential Information.

(c) **Non-Use; Non-Disclosure.** I will not, during or after my engagement, (i) use Confidential Information for any purpose other than performing my duties for the Company, or (ii) disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written authorization.

(d) **Permitted Disclosures.** Notwithstanding the foregoing, I may disclose Confidential Information (i) as required by law (with prior notice to the Company where lawful), or (ii) to my legal, tax, or financial advisors who are bound by confidentiality obligations.

(e) **Defend Trade Secrets Act Notice.** I understand that an individual shall not be held criminally or civilly liable under any federal or state trade-secret law for the disclosure of a trade secret that is made (i) in confidence to a federal, state, or local government official or to an attorney solely for the purpose of reporting or investigating a suspected violation of law; or (ii) in a complaint or other document filed under seal in a lawsuit or other proceeding. (Defend Trade Secrets Act of 2016, 18 U.S.C. § 1833.)

### 2. Inventions

(a) **Definitions.**
- "**Invention**" means any invention, discovery, improvement, design, original work of authorship, formula, process, computer program, database, technique, know-how, or trade secret, whether or not patentable or registrable under copyright or similar laws.
- "**Company Invention**" means any Invention that I conceive, develop, or reduce to practice (whether alone or with others), during the period of my engagement and for the period of [3] months thereafter, that (i) results from work performed for the Company, (ii) uses Company time, equipment, supplies, facilities, or Confidential Information, or (iii) relates at the time of conception or reduction to practice to the Company's business or to the Company's actual or demonstrably anticipated research or development.

(b) **Prior Inventions.** I have listed on **Exhibit A** all Inventions made prior to my engagement that I claim as my own and that should be excluded from this Agreement ("Prior Inventions"). If no Exhibit A is attached, or if I check the box on Exhibit A indicating "No Prior Inventions," I represent that I have no Prior Inventions.

(c) **Assignment of Company Inventions.** I hereby assign and agree to assign to the Company all of my right, title, and interest in and to all Company Inventions, including all Intellectual Property Rights in such Company Inventions.

(d) **License Back of Necessary Prior Inventions.** If, in the course of my engagement, I incorporate any Prior Invention into a Company product or process, I hereby grant the Company a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free, perpetual, irrevocable, sublicensable license to use such Prior Invention for any purpose.

(e) **Moral Rights Waiver.** To the maximum extent permitted by law, I waive all moral rights in any Company Invention.

(f) **Cooperation.** I will execute (and assist the Company in executing and prosecuting) all documents necessary to apply for, obtain, and enforce intellectual-property rights in any Company Invention. The Company is hereby appointed my attorney-in-fact for the limited purpose of executing such documents if I fail to do so.

(g) **California / State Notice.** *(For California residents only — replace or remove for other jurisdictions.)* This assignment does not apply to any invention that I developed entirely on my own time without using the Company's equipment, supplies, facilities, or trade-secret information, except for those inventions that either (i) relate at the time of conception or reduction to practice to the Company's business or to the Company's actual or demonstrably anticipated research or development, or (ii) result from any work performed by me for the Company. (California Labor Code § 2870.) Equivalent statutes apply in Delaware, Illinois, Kansas, Minnesota, North Carolina, Utah, and Washington — adapt as required.

### 3. Pre-Existing Obligations and Third-Party Rights

I represent and warrant that:
(a) I am not subject to any agreement that conflicts with this Agreement or my engagement with the Company.
(b) I will not bring to the Company, or use in my work for the Company, any confidential information or trade secrets of any prior employer or other third party.
(c) I will not enter into any agreement during my engagement that conflicts with this Agreement.

### 4. Return of Property

Upon termination of my engagement (or earlier upon request), I will promptly return to the Company all Company property in my possession, including documents, devices, source code, and any media containing Confidential Information. I will not retain any copies.

### 5. Non-Solicitation

For a period of **[12] months** after the termination of my engagement, I will not solicit any employee or contractor of the Company to leave the Company. *(Non-solicitation clauses must be drafted to comply with state law — California prohibits employee non-solicits; the FTC's stance on non-competes also affects breadth. Confirm with counsel.)*

### 6. No Conflicting Activities

During my engagement, I will not engage in any business activity that competes with, or that is otherwise inconsistent with, my obligations to the Company, without the Company's prior written consent.

### 7. At-Will Employment / Engagement

If I am an employee, my employment is at-will and may be terminated at any time, with or without cause or notice, by either me or the Company.

### 8. General Provisions

(a) **Governing Law.** This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of **[Delaware / California]**, without regard to its conflict-of-laws principles.

(b) **Equitable Relief.** I acknowledge that any breach of this Agreement may cause the Company irreparable harm, and that the Company is entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to damages.

(c) **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with any offer letter, RSPA, or option agreement, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to its subject matter.

(d) **Severability.** If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

(e) **Counterparts; Electronic Signature.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature.
