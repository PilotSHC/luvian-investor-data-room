## RESTRICTED STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT

**This Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement** (this "**Agreement**") is made as of **[YYYY-MM-DD]** (the "**Grant Date**") by and between **Luvian, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation (the "**Company**"), and **[FOUNDER_NAME]** ("**Purchaser**").

### Recitals

WHEREAS, the Company desires to issue and sell to Purchaser, and Purchaser desires to acquire from the Company, shares of the Company's common stock subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

---

### 1. Sale of Stock

(a) **Issuance.** The Company hereby issues and sells to Purchaser, and Purchaser hereby purchases from the Company, **[NUMBER] shares** (the "**Shares**") of the Company's common stock, par value $0.00001 per share ("**Common Stock**"), at a purchase price of **$0.00001 per share**, for an aggregate purchase price of **$[AGGREGATE_PRICE]** (the "**Purchase Price**"), which Purchaser shall pay by check, wire transfer, cash, or services rendered, on or before the Grant Date.

(b) **Issuance of Stock.** The Shares shall be issued in book-entry form on the records of the Company's transfer agent (or, if none, the Company's stock ledger).

### 2. Vesting

(a) **Vesting Schedule.** Subject to Section 2(b), the Shares shall vest as follows:

- **25% (a "Cliff")** on the first anniversary of the **Vesting Commencement Date** (defined below).
- The remaining **75%** shall vest in **36 equal monthly installments** over the **36 months following** the first anniversary of the Vesting Commencement Date.

The "**Vesting Commencement Date**" is **[YYYY-MM-DD]** (typically the date Purchaser commenced services to the Company, which may pre-date incorporation).

(b) **Continuous Service.** Vesting shall cease immediately upon termination of Purchaser's continuous service relationship (as employee, consultant, director, or officer) with the Company for any reason.

### 3. Repurchase Option

(a) **Right of Repurchase.** If Purchaser's continuous service relationship with the Company terminates for any reason (including death, disability, or termination with or without cause), the Company shall have the option (the "**Repurchase Option**"), exercisable for 90 days following such termination, to repurchase any **Unvested Shares** at a price equal to the original Purchase Price per share.

(b) **Vested Shares.** Vested Shares are not subject to the Repurchase Option but are subject to the right of first refusal in Section 5.

(c) **Acceleration on Change of Control.** If, within 12 months following a **Change of Control** of the Company, Purchaser's service relationship is terminated by the Company (or its successor) **without Cause** or by Purchaser **for Good Reason**, then **100% of the then-Unvested Shares shall accelerate** and become vested as of the date of such termination ("**double-trigger acceleration**").

### 4. Definitions

(a) "**Cause**" means (i) willful misconduct or gross negligence; (ii) material breach of this Agreement, the CIIA, or any other written agreement with the Company; (iii) commission of a felony or crime of moral turpitude; (iv) material violation of Company policy after written notice and a reasonable cure period.

(b) "**Good Reason**" means, after written notice to the Company and a 30-day cure period, (i) a material reduction in title, duties, or authority; (ii) a material reduction in base compensation; (iii) a relocation of Purchaser's principal place of work by more than 50 miles.

(c) "**Change of Control**" means (i) a merger, consolidation, or reorganization in which the Company's stockholders before the transaction hold less than 50% of the voting securities of the surviving entity afterward; or (ii) a sale of all or substantially all of the Company's assets.

### 5. Right of First Refusal

Before any sale or transfer of Vested Shares to a third party, Purchaser shall first offer such shares to the Company on the same terms (the "**ROFR**"). If the Company does not exercise the ROFR within 30 days, Purchaser may sell to the third party on the same terms within 90 days.

The ROFR shall not apply to transfers to immediate family members or to a trust for estate-planning purposes, provided the transferee agrees in writing to be bound by this Agreement.

### 6. Lock-Up

In connection with the Company's initial public offering, Purchaser will not sell, transfer, or otherwise dispose of any Shares for a period of up to 180 days following the offering, as requested by the underwriters.

### 7. 83(b) Election

(a) **Filing.** Purchaser is strongly advised to file an election under Section 83(b) of the Internal Revenue Code with the Internal Revenue Service within 30 days of the Grant Date. **Failure to file timely will result in adverse tax consequences.**

(b) **Form.** A form of 83(b) election is attached as **Exhibit A** (see `83b_Election_TEMPLATE.md`).

(c) **Acknowledgment.** Purchaser acknowledges that the responsibility for filing the 83(b) election is solely Purchaser's, and that the Company has not provided tax advice.

### 8. Investment Representations

Purchaser represents that:
(a) Purchaser is acquiring the Shares for investment for Purchaser's own account, and not with a view to resale or distribution.
(b) Purchaser has had access to all information about the Company that Purchaser has requested.
(c) Purchaser is an accredited investor or otherwise qualifies for an exemption from registration.
(d) Purchaser understands the Shares have not been registered under the Securities Act and will be subject to transfer restrictions.

### 9. Legends

The book-entry record (or certificate, if any) for the Shares shall bear legends substantially in the form:

> THE SHARES REPRESENTED HEREBY HAVE NOT BEEN REGISTERED UNDER THE SECURITIES ACT OF 1933, AS AMENDED, OR ANY STATE SECURITIES LAW, AND MAY NOT BE TRANSFERRED EXCEPT IN COMPLIANCE WITH SUCH LAWS.

> THE SHARES REPRESENTED HEREBY ARE SUBJECT TO TRANSFER AND VESTING RESTRICTIONS SET FORTH IN A RESTRICTED STOCK PURCHASE AGREEMENT, A COPY OF WHICH IS AVAILABLE AT THE COMPANY'S OFFICES.

### 10. General Provisions

(a) **Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict-of-laws principles.

(b) **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with the CIIA and any related plan documents, constitutes the entire agreement of the parties with respect to its subject matter.

(c) **Amendment.** No amendment shall be effective unless in writing and signed by both parties.

(d) **Counterparts; Electronic Signature.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts and by electronic signature (e.g., DocuSign), each of which shall constitute an original.

(e) **Severability.** If any provision is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.
